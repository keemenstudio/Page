Game on Steam

DETECTIVE: THE MOUNTAIN CITY (Steam) is a 3D pixel-art puzzle adventure game. In this game, players play as a detective who newly arrived in the mountain city, helping the local police solve the crime. Players need to be able to use their powers of deduction to find the killers and bring them to justice. Players are searching for evidence at the crime scene, analyzing clues and finding the hidden stories behind each murder.

I planned for this game for over three years and went through several iterations. Game development was completely my personal interest and not related to my current career. Starting the first Unity3D tutorial on YouTube in 2018, I self-learnt all the related skills to develop games. Including Unity3D Engine, game design, programming in C#, pixel artwork, Blender 3d modelling, and publishing.

Later, I recruited members of my team who shared the same interests and passions in gaming and assigned different tasks to them based on their specialization. Currently, we have four team members working on this project and planning to lunch the game in 2023.

You may find more information on: https://store.steampowered.com/app/2002310/Detective_The_Mountain_City/
