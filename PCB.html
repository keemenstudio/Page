High Voltage DC-AC Inverter

I designed and built a high voltage DC (200V) to AC half-bridge power inverter with my teammates for the undergraduate capstone project. Our client was professor Dr.Gregory Kish at the University of Alberta. He wanted to do some research on the conversion from high voltage DC to AC.

We researched the DC to AC inverter by a MOSFET half bridge, shoot through protection, miller clamp and overcurrent protection.

We selected SiC MOSFET(C3M0120065K), electrolytic capacitor banks(381LX331M200J012), and gate drivers(UCC21732DW). We designed the protection circuits around the gate driver to prevent overcurrent or shorted.

I designed an industrial standard PCB layout for this circuit and received tremendous support from a professional engineer, Albert Terheide.

Acknowledgement: I would like to express my special thanks of gratitude to Dr.Gregory Kish, Albert Terheide, Sheron Bolonne and Anjana Wijesekera.
